# Privacy Policy for PromptFlow Pro

**Last Updated:** May 2026

PromptFlow Pro ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

### 1. Data Collection & Storage
PromptFlow Pro is designed to be privacy-first. 
* **Local Storage:** All of your saved prompts, folders, tags, and analytics are stored strictly locally on your device using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. We do not maintain any external databases or cloud servers to store your personal data.
* **No Account Required:** You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or log in to use the extension.

### 2. How We Use Your Data (Website Content)
In order to provide our core features (saving prompts, enhancing text, and exporting chats), the extension must read the text you type or the text displayed on the active webpage.
* **AI Enhancement:** When you explicitly click the "Enhance" button, the text you entered is sent anonymously to our third-party AI provider (Pollinations.ai) solely for the purpose of rewriting and optimizing your prompt. It is not tied to your identity.
* **Chat Export:** When you explicitly click "Download Chat", the extension reads the active chat window to generate a Markdown file. This file is downloaded directly to your local computer and is never transmitted to us.

### 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We **do not** sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information or website content to outside parties for marketing, advertising, or data brokering purposes. 

### 4. Third-Party Services
We utilize a free, anonymous endpoint via Pollinations.ai for the "Enhance Prompt" feature. By using this feature, your prompt text is temporarily processed by their API to return an enhanced result. Please review their privacy policy if you have concerns regarding their data handling.

### 5. Your Consent
By using PromptFlow Pro, you consent to our privacy policy.

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us via our GitHub Repository Issues page: https://github.com/harshraj0235/promptflow/issues
